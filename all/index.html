About Us

Wassalni: Simplifying Student Transportation to and from University

Hello! We would like to announce the establishment of Wassalni, a company dedicated to developing a specialized website for students, aimed at facilitating transportation to and from the university. The website will be professionally designed and available in English. The website features several functionalities to ensure a comfortable and efficient experience for students. An interactive map will be provided, displaying the locations of parking lots, trams, and various stations within the university area. This map will be regularly updated to ensure the availability of up-to-date and accurate information. Best regards,The Wassalni Team
